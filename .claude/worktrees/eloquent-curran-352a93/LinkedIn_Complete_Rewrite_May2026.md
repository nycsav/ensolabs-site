## Research Summary: What Actually Works on LinkedIn in 2026

Before the copy, here's what the research says:

**Headline:** LinkedIn now gives you 220 characters. First 80 chars show on mobile search and recruiter previews — put the most important keywords there. Best formula: Role | Who You Help + What Outcome | Credential. Including "AI Transformation" is critical — it's the #2 fastest-growing job keyword on LinkedIn in 2026, right behind "AI Engineer."

**About section — the "above the fold" rule:** Only 275 characters show before "See more" on mobile. If your hook doesn't land in those 275 characters, most people never read the rest. Best-performing profiles use: 1-2 sentence hook → short paragraph of context → bullet points for proof → CTA.

**Format:** Hybrid — short paragraphs for narrative, bullet points for achievements and metrics. Never a wall of text. Double line breaks between sections. Total length: 1,200–1,800 characters optimal.

**Algorithm:** LinkedIn switched to semantic (LLM-based) retrieval in Feb 2026. It reads meaning, not keyword density. But cross-validation still matters — skills in your Skills section should also appear naturally in your About and Experience.

**Top-performing consultant profiles** follow this pattern: "I help [audience] [achieve outcome]" → 2-3 proof points with metrics → credentials → CTA.

Sources: [Resume Vera 2026 Guide](https://resumevera.com/blogs/linkedin-profile-optimization-guide-2026) · [Teal HQ AI Consultant Guide](https://www.tealhq.com/linkedin-guides/ai-consultant) · [Resume Worded Consultant Examples](https://resumeworded.com/linkedin-samples/consultant-linkedin-summary-examples) · [Meet Sona Founders Guide](https://meetsona.ai/blog/linkedin-profile-optimization-founders-consultants/) · [OphyAI Recruiter-Tested Fixes](https://ophyai.com/blog/resume-writing/linkedin-profile-optimization-job-search) · [Jobscan Headline Formulas](https://www.jobscan.co/blog/impactful-linkedin-headline-examples/)

**Why this structure works:**
- **First 275 chars (above the fold):** "I help enterprise teams turn AI strategy into production systems that actually ship. Most organizations are stuck between 'we have an AI roadmap' and 'we have a running system.'" — this is what shows before "See more." It names the audience, the problem, and hints at the solution.
