## Positioning Thesis

The through-line across 15 years isn't "strategist who learned AI." It's the same role, evolved: the person in the room who understands the business problem AND builds the system that solves it. At Rokkan it was AmEx digital. At BBDO it was the AT&T campaign architecture. At RAPP it was Pfizer CRM systems. Now it's LangGraph pipelines, MCP servers, and RAG systems — but the job is the same: sit across from the buyer, understand the problem, build the thing.

What makes you hireable for a 3-month contract isn't "C-suite advisory" — it's that you walk in Monday, understand the room because you've spent 15 years in rooms like it, and start building on Tuesday. Most AI engineers can't navigate the client meeting. Most strategists can't write the code. You do both. That's the positioning.
