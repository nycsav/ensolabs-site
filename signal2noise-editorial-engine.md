## Content Convergence Pattern
The most powerful signals combine TWO news items into ONE Enso angle. Examples:

- Claude Finance Agents (May 5) + Perplexity Finance Search = "The financial AI stack is consolidating around Claude and Perplexity. We've been building on both for 6 months. Here's what the convergence means."
- MCP Ecosystem Growth + Enterprise Agent Adoption = "80% of enterprise apps now embed agents. MCP is the protocol that connects them. We build MCP servers as 2-6 week engagements."
- AI Governance Gap + Claude Managed Agent Memory = "Only 20% of companies have AI governance. Claude just shipped agent memory with audit logs. The window to get governance right is now."

## Editorial Voice
- Analytical: Lead with data, not opinion
- Practitioner: Speak from building experience, not research
- Direct: Name winners/losers and tradeoffs
- Connected: Every insight ties back to work Enso has done or can do
- No emojis, no hype language, no hashtags in post body

## Post Structure
1. **Hook** — Most surprising data point or convergence (1 line)
2. **The Signal** — 2-3 sentences of synthesis with source attribution
3. **The Enso Take** — 1-2 sentences of practitioner POV from actual engagements
4. **Your Monday Move** — One specific, actionable step
5. **Link** — ensolabs.ai/insights/signals/[slug]
6. **Footer** — signal2noise by Enso Labs
